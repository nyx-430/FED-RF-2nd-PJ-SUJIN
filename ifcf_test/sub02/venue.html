Conrad Seoul

About Venue Hotel

The Conrad Seoul Hotel is a 5-star hotel located in Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul. It is connected to IFC Mall and is within four blocks of Yeouido subway station, which connects to central Seoul. There are several tourist attractions nearby such as the 63 Building, Yeouido Han River Park, andthe National Assembly Building. The high- end business hotel provides a wide range of excellent meeting and banquet spaces spread over three floors.

Nearby Tourist Attractions
IFC Mall
The IFC Mall is a large indoor mall nearest Conrad Seoul. There is a diverse range of stores that sell clothing, cosmetics, international designer brands, and more.
The Hyundai Seoul
The Hyundai Seoul is the largest shopping mall in Seoul, covering an area the size of 13 football fields. There are many attractive spaces like gardens and water foundations. Such elements offer visitors a chance to enjoy a unique and relaxed shopping experience.
Yeouido Han River Park
The Han River is the most important physical landmark in the city. It is the perfect place to picnic with Korean-style fried chicken. Also, people can rent bikes, go on a cruise, or see the night view.
National Assembly Building
The National Assembly Building houses Korea's legislative branch of government and it is in Yeouido. The building represents the democracy and human rights of Korea.
