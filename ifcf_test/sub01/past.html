The International Financial Cooperation Forum (IFCF) is jointly hosted by the Financial Services Commission of Korea and CIFC. The IFCF is hosted annually to bring together financial leaders from around the world to share insights and experiences to broaden the understanding of the changing financial environment and to deepen global financial cooperation.

Over the last seven IFCF, CIFC and its global partners have forged valuable long-term relationships for sharing financial knowledge and strengthening cooperation. CIFC sincerely believes that the 8th IFCF will serve as a guide in overcoming new challenges ahead for the global financial community.

The 8th IFCF is scheduled for December 4, 2023 and is organized under the theme “Trends in Global Financial Cooperation for Sustainability.” The forum aims to be a venue for personal networking opportunities for all participants to ultimately promote financial cooperation.

The IFCF is a 4-day long program that consists of an all-day forum, a networking session, Korean counterpart meeting, an optional cultural exploration, and a farewell dinner.

We look forward to your participation and support.

2014: The 1st IFCF
Strengthening Financial Infrastructure: Towards Shared Growth of Financial Markets in Asia
The 1st IFCF titled “Strengthening Financial Infrastructure: Towards Shared Growth of Financial Markets in Asia” hosted in December 2014, gathered government officials from Pan-Asian region and representatives from the International Monetary Fund and Asian Development Bank to heighten financial cooperation in the Asian region.
2015: The 2nd IFCF
The Launch of ASEAN Economic Community and its Future Prospects
The 2nd IFCF hosted in December 2015 under the topic “The Launch of ASEAN Economic Community and its Future Prospects” invited government officials and financial experts from ASEAN member countries, the World Bank Group and Asian Development Bank to discuss how to strengthen economic growth and financial infrastructure of the ASEAN countries.
2016: The 3rd IFCF
Financial Innovation within Reach
The 3rd IFCF organized under the theme “Financial Innovation within Reach” in December 2016 examined the efforts to accelerate financial innovation and innovative financial systems of the future with government and industry leaders from 12 countries including the World Bank and the European Commission.
2017: The 4th IFCF
Key Financial Drivers for the Era of Cooperation and Shared Prosperity
The 4th IFCF examined the areas of artificial intelligence, big data and the Internet of Things in the Insurance Industry and blockchain in the financial industry under the title “Key Financial Drivers for the Era of Cooperation and Shared Prosperity”.
2018: The 5th IFCF
The Launch of ASEAN Economic Community and its Future Prospects
The 5th IFCF, “Harnessing Financial Technology to Promote Financial Inclusion” provided an in-depth review of the progress of financial inclusion and types of fintech influencing different areas of the financial sector.
2019: The 6th IFCF
Embracing Digital Transformation: The Future of the Financial Industry
The 6th IFCF organized under the theme “Embracing Digital Transformation: The Future of the Financial Industry” in December 2019 shared insights on digital transformation and addressed major regulatory challenges.
2023: The 7th IFCF
Global Financial Cooperation in the Digital Transformation Era
The 7th IFCF hosted in February 20th, 2023 under the title "Global Financial Cooperation in the Digital Transformation Era" invited government officials and financial experts from eight-teen foreign countries to discuss the digital transformation in the finance area and celebrated the 10th anniversary of the establishment of CIFC.
2023: The 8th IFCF
The Launch of ASEAN Economic Community and its Future Prospects
In the 8th IFCF, hosted on 4 December 2024 under the theme 'Trends in Global Financial cooperation for Sustainability,' the financial leaders from around the world shared their best practices and experiences in sustainable finance and financial infrastructure for sustainability.
